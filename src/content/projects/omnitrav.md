OmniTrav studies camera-to-traversability estimation from 360° visual observations for embodied intelligence and mobile robot navigation.

The project describes a large-scale dataset with approximately 4.7k scenes, 250k frames, and 577 GB of data, together with model designs involving DINOv3, FPN, and cross-attention angle projection.
